# JobVisor — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 12, 2026**

JobVisor is a Chrome extension that helps job seekers — especially international students — evaluate LinkedIn job postings by showing quick job overview, H-1B sponsorship history, an ATS resume match score, and personalized resume recommendations.

This policy explains what data JobVisor handles, where it goes, and what we never do with it.

\---

## 1\. Data We Handle

### Your resume

* When you upload your resume, its text is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
* Your resume is **never uploaded to our servers** — we do not operate servers that store user data.
* Portions of your resume text are sent to an AI processing service (see Section 2) **only at the moment you analyze a job**, solely to compute your match score and recommendations.

### Job posting content

* When you view a LinkedIn job page, JobVisor reads the publicly visible job description on that page to analyze it.
* This content is processed to generate the analysis shown in the overlay.

### License key (Pro users)

* If you purchase a Pro subscription, your license key is stored locally on your device.
* Payment is processed entirely by our payment provider (Lemon Squeezy). **We never see or store your payment card details.**

### Analysis results

* Analysis results are cached locally on your device so repeat visits to the same job are instant and consistent.

\---

## 2\. Third-Party Services

JobVisor sends data to the following services strictly to provide its core functionality:

|Service|What is sent|Why|
|-|-|-|
|AI processing API (Anthropic Claude)|Job description text and relevant portions of your resume text|To generate the job analysis, ATS score, and recommendations|
|Lemon Squeezy|License key validation requests|To verify Pro subscription status|

* AI providers process this data to return the analysis and are not authorized by us to use it for other purposes. Refer to [Anthropic's privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy) for their data handling practices.
* We do not send your name, email, or any account identifiers with analysis requests.

\---

## 3\. What We Never Do

* We **never sell** your data to anyone.
* We **never share** your resume or job activity with advertisers, recruiters, or data brokers.
* We **never store** your resume or analysis history on our own servers.
* We **never read** LinkedIn pages other than the job postings you actively view.
* We **never collect** your LinkedIn credentials, messages, connections, or profile data.

\---

## 4\. Data Retention \& Deletion

All JobVisor data lives on your own device. You are in full control:

* **Delete your resume:** remove or replace it any time from the extension popup.
* **Delete everything:** uninstalling the extension permanently removes all stored data, including your resume, cached analyses, and license key.
* You can also clear the extension's storage from `chrome://extensions` → JobVisor → Details → Clear data (where available in your Chrome version).

We retain nothing after deletion because we hold nothing in the first place.

\---

## 5\. Permissions Explained

JobVisor requests the minimum Chrome permissions needed to function:

* **Access to linkedin.com job pages** — to read the job description you're viewing and display the analysis overlay.
* **Storage** — to save your resume, settings, and cached analyses locally on your device.

JobVisor does not request access to your browsing history, other websites, or any data beyond what is listed above.

\---

## 6\. Children's Privacy

JobVisor is intended for job seekers and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

\---

## 7\. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Material changes (such as introducing server-side accounts) will be highlighted in the extension's update notes.
